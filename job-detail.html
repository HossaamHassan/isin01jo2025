Job Description

We are looking for an experienced Senior Claims Specialist to join our Claims Department. The ideal candidate will be responsible for processing, assessing, and managing insurance claims in accordance with Takaful principles and company policies. This position requires excellent analytical skills, attention to detail, and strong customer service experience.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Review and process insurance claims
  • Assess claim validity and determine appropriate coverage
  • Explore complex claims and coordinate with relevant parties.
  • Manage insurance claims according to Takaful principles and company policies.
  • Explore complex claims and coordinate with relevant parties.

Requirements & Qualifications

Requirements:

  • Grade of Bachelor's in Insurance or Business Administration or related field
  • Experience of at least 5 years in processing insurance claims
  • Excellent analytical skills
  • Good communication skills
  • Experience in processing insurance claims

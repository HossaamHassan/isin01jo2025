Islamic Insurance Company Signs Agreement with King Hussein Cancer Foundation

Aug 17, 2025

Amman, May 2025: Islamic Insurance Company proudly announces the signing of a strategic partnership agreement with King Hussein Cancer Foundation (KHCF). This collaboration aims to provide comprehensive health insurance coverage for cancer patients and ensure access to world-class treatment facilities. Under this agreement, policyholders of Islamic Insurance Company will benefit from direct billing services at the King Hussein Cancer Center, streamlined claim processes, and access to specialized oncology services. This partnership reflects the company's commitment to supporting the healthcare needs of the Jordanian community and providing Takaful insurance solutions that make a real difference in people's lives during their most challenging times.
